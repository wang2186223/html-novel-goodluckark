The crowd hurled furious accusations at Eleanor.

Eleanor's heart already shattered from the current crisis, now faced relentless scorn from those who once curried her favor.

They were the same individuals who previously flattered her and sought her friendship.

Yet now...

It was Victoria! That deceitful Victoria!

She seized Senior Montgomery's thirty percent company shares and liquidated everything, creating this chaos!

How could she be so utterly heartless? What possible motive drove her to such betrayal?

Adrian slipped away from the hospital room amidst the turmoil.

Outside the hospital, he pulled out his phone to contact Isabella. "Isabella, should I reveal the truth to them now?"

"Not yet. Let them discover it gradually—they'll learn everything in due time. For now, stay by Grandpa's side personally these next few days. Ensure he receives proper care. Also, cover Benjamin's medical expenses on my behalf."

Isabella was resting in her room during the call. She attempted to sleep after ending the conversation.

But her mind swirled with too many thoughts, keeping her awake. She had anticipated this moment for five long years.

Since everything had descended into chaos, so be it. She wanted the entire Montgomery family to witness their beloved Victoria's true nature!

Had they not mocked her for being the adopted daughter and demanded she never set foot on Montgomery property again?

She longed to see their expressions when the truth emerged—Isabella wanted to hear what excuses they could possibly offer!

The following day unfolded exactly as predicted. The Montgomery family descended into total disarray.

All of William and Beatrice's assets had been sold, leaving them completely homeless. The hospital became their only shelter, though the remaining funds in their bank accounts were severely limited.

After covering the exorbitant medical costs, they struggled to afford even basic meals.

When the bank discovered the fifty-billion-dollar loan had vanished, they immediately shuttered every Montgomery Group factory, along with all their hotels and properties.

They issued a five-day ultimatum for debt repayment before initiating asset liquidation.

The Montgomerys' relatives and friends, terrified of being entangled in the disaster, fled instantly. None wished to be associated with the catastrophe.

Even all the household staff abandoned the manor. Senior Montgomery, Richard, Gregory, Penelope, and the rest remained confined to the hospital, surviving on inexpensive hospital food.

Overnight, the Montgomery family's glory evaporated, replaced by desperate circumstances.

Every financial news outlet and social media platform exploded with headlines:

[Montgomery Group Shuts Down Operations!]

[Montgomery Family Swamped with 50 Billion Debt!]

[Victoria Kensington Absconds with Family Fortune—Manhunt Underway!]

Comment sections flooded with shock and disbelief.

[Unbelievable! Victoria actually stole their money and ran!]

[That innocent-faced Victoria committed such an act?]

[Isn't she the Montgomery heiress? Even without stealing, she controlled sixty percent of shares. Why would she do this?]

[She fled with the money, making herself universally despised. Is she insane?]

[This year's most sensational drama! Can't wait for the full truth to emerge!]

The public watched the spectacle with morbid fascination. Some investigators dug into the Montgomery family history, uncovering their profound foolishness—they were arguably the most gullible family imaginable.

Victoria had been their favored child since infancy. They invested enormous sums in her upbringing, enrolling her in piano lessons, language courses, and countless other trainings.

Their expenditure on her far exceeded what they spent on their other children. What could have driven her to such betrayal?

BANG!

A sudden, explosive noise reverberated through the hospital ward.
