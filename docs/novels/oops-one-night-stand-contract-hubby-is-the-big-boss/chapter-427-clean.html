Chapter 427

"You think you're invincible just because you killed a few ants, Lin Feng?"

Tian Chen's eyes flashed with cold light.

A murderous aura surged from his entire body.

The surrounding temperature instantly dropped to freezing.

"Indeed, I do believe I am quite formidable."

Lin Feng's tone was indifferent.

He immediately struck back with a palm aimed at Tian Chen.

Su Wenxiang and Su Yuqing both gasped sharply.

If this palm strike landed, Tian Chen would be unable to block it.

They would undoubtedly die.

Tian Chen remained completely still.

Only when the palm force neared his face did a semi-transparent barrier suddenly appear around him.

"Boom!"

The palm force violently collided with the barrier.

The barrier distorted intensely but absorbed all the energy.

Lin Feng's gaze sharpened slightly.

This defensive artifact was extraordinary.

It could completely neutralize his attack.

"Merely a Nascent Soul cultivator. If not for other reasons, I would have crushed you long ago."

"Since you wish to play, I shall indulge you. Dare to follow me? I will show you what true power is!"

Tian Chen sneered coldly.

He flung Su Wenxiang and Su Yuqing aside.

He transformed into a streak of light and shot into the sky.

Su Yuqing collapsed to the ground.

The drug had already invaded her heart meridian.

Her eyes were dazed.

She moaned unconsciously, "Hot... so hot..."

"Lin Feng, Yuqing has been poisoned with an aphrodisiac! Only you can save her!"

Su Wenxiang shouted urgently.

If they could cling to Lin Feng, the Su family would surely rise.

"Dream on."

Lin Feng uttered two words.

He transformed into a streak of light and gave chase.

No matter how beautiful a woman was, she couldn't compare to the temptation of a Soul Formation cultivator.

...

Moments later, the two landed one after another in a desolate mountain forest.

The bright moon hung high in the sky.

Silver radiance showered down, illuminating the entire forest as if it were daytime.

Tian Chen stood with his hands behind his back.

He looked down at Lin Feng as if observing an ant.

"Lin Feng, do you know what realm I have reached?"

Tian Chen's tone was playful.

He was in no hurry to attack.

He enjoyed the thrill of this cat-and-mouse game.

"Early stage Soul Formation."

Lin Feng responded flatly.

"Since you know, how dare you still follow me?"

Tian Chen raised an eyebrow.

"Too much nonsense!"

Lin Feng sneered and struck out with another palm.

The semi-transparent barrier appeared again, blocking the attack.

Lin Feng's gaze fell on Tian Chen's white shirt.

This magical robe was at least a high-grade spiritual artifact.

Its origins were certainly not simple.

"See? You can't even break my defense."

"A casual strike from me can take your life."

"But don't worry, I won't kill you now."

"Tonight, I will make you understand that there is always someone stronger!"

Tian Chen formed hand seals with both hands.

A terrifying beam of light shot from his palms, directly blasting toward Lin Feng.

Lin Feng neither dodged nor avoided it.

He took the blow head-on.

His upper garment instantly shattered, revealing his well-built torso.

"Good. You are the first person to make me feel pain."

Before his words faded, Lin Feng appeared in front of Tian Chen.

He threw a punch.

"I have the Divine Silk Robe protecting me! You cannot break my defense!"

Tian Chen laughed coldly.

The barrier appeared once more.

But it distorted violently under the force of the punch.

Finally, it shattered with a crisp cracking sound.

The punch continued its momentum.

It landed heavily on Tian Chen's abdomen.

"Boom!"

Caught off guard, Tian Chen was sent flying backward.

He smashed into the mountain, leaving a human-shaped crater.

"Show me some real skill. You cannot win against me relying solely on a magical robe."

Lin Feng slowly retracted his fist.

"Whoosh!"

Tian Chen shot out from the crater.

He hovered in mid-air.

He wiped the blood from the corner of his mouth.

His expression was extremely grim.

He had miscalculated.

He never expected his opponent to be skilled in both magical and physical cultivation.

"Interesting. I underestimated you."

"From now on, you won't have any more opportunities!"

Tian Chen formed hand seals with both hands.

Terrifying energy accumulated in his palms.

Brilliant light illuminated the entire forest.

It even outshone the moonlight.

"Lin Feng, taste the power of my Joyful Union Sect's Divine Flame Art!"
