Chapter 163

Hearing Finn Carter's inner thoughts, Aria Stewart knew she had lost again.

His meaning of "big" certainly wasn't about age.

Her heart instantly sank into an icy abyss.

She didn't care about Finn Carter.

Losing to her younger sister Luna Stewart once more just made her feel utterly hopeless about her own life.

"Old Master, may I speak with Miss Qingxuan alone?" Finn Carter suddenly turned to look at Luna Stewart, his tone calm.

The Old Master Lin understood, thinking Finn Carter had made his choice in this manner.

"Fine, as you wish."

When Finn Carter and Luna Stewart walked out the door, Aria Stewart finally couldn't hold on any longer.

She slowly crouched down.

Old Master Lin looked at his granddaughter with a pained heart, but didn't know how to comfort her.

He could only sigh helplessly.

......

Finn Carter and Luna Stewart walked all the way to the sanitarium garden.

Luna Stewart kept holding Finn Carter's arm the entire time, smiling radiantly.

"Miss Lin, your sister can't see us anymore." Finn Carter stopped walking and spoke plainly.

Luna Stewart was startled.

She gave a charming, silky-eyed laugh. "Isn't holding you comfortable? Your words say no, but your body says yes!"

Finn Carter was no fool.

He had long seen the terrible relationship between the two sisters.

He wasn't narcissistic enough to think Luna Stewart had fallen for him at first sight.

She was clearly using him to provoke her sister.

Finn Carter smiled.

He suddenly reached out and tore off the earring from Luna Stewart's left ear.

"What are you doing!" Luna Stewart's face instantly changed.

She covered her ear and scolded angrily. "This is a rare sapphire! Selling you wouldn't cover the cost! Give it back to me now! Did you really think a girl like me would be interested in a poor security guard like you?!"

Finn Carter glanced at her calmly.

He slapped her rounded bottom. "Quiet down!"

Instantly, waves rippled.

So springy, so soft!

"Ah!" Luna Stewart cried out softly, both embarrassed and furious. "Bastard! How dare you hit me......"

Finn Carter ignored her.

He pinched the sapphire between his thumb and middle finger and gave a light squeeze.

"Crack!"

The gemstone split into two halves with a crisp sound.

Just as Luna Stewart was about to erupt, she saw Finn Carter pick up a tiny device from the fragments.

"What is this?" Luna Stewart asked in surprise.

"A listening device." Finn Carter said gravely. "RK800, the latest model."

"A listening device?!" Luna Stewart covered her mouth, her beautiful face pale with fright. "How could......"

"Check who gave you these earrings." Finn Carter said coolly.

Luna Stewart's body trembled. "It was him......"

Before she could recover, a wisp of golden flame suddenly emerged from Finn Carter's palm.

With a sizzling sound, the listening device turned to ashes, scattering with the wind.

"You...... you......" Luna Stewart pointed at Finn Carter, speechless.

This was simply not something an ordinary person could do!

"Helping you once out of respect for the Old Master." Finn Carter looked at her. "Your sisterly issues have nothing to do with me. Don't use me as your pawn."

After speaking, he turned and left.

Leaving Luna Stewart standing frozen in place, nearly petrified.

......

In the driver's seat of the Lamborghini, Aria Stewart leaned over the steering wheel, crying silently.

She felt utterly desperate and lonely.

She had been crying for over an hour.

To avoid worrying her grandfather, she could only hide in the car and cry secretly.

Suddenly, the car door opened.

Someone got into the passenger seat.

Aria Stewart was startled.

When she saw it was Finn Carter, she flew into a rage. "Get out!"

Finn Carter didn't speak.

He just looked at her quietly.

Sigh... This poor older woman.

From what her mother said, she's being forced into a business marriage.

The company she built with her own hands will be taken away and given to her sister.

That sister is no easy person either, constantly causing trouble for her.

The only one who dotes on her, her grandfather, is making her marry a stranger.

Sigh... Whatever. A live-in marriage it is.

I'll be the live-in son-in-law for a couple days, then divorce later.

Who told me I'm soft-hearted and can't stand to see a woman cry!

Hearing Finn Carter's inner thoughts, Aria Stewart was stunned.

She never expected that in the end, it would be this security guard she looked down on who understood her grievances the most.

This understanding became her final outlet for release.

She could no longer hold back.

She buried her face in the steering wheel and wept openly.

"Stop crying. The first milk tea of autumn, want some?" Finn Carter handed over a paper bag.

Aria Stewart lifted her head, tears streaming down her face like rain on pear blossoms.

Her long eyelashes were wet with teardrops, a pitiful sight.

She snatched the milk tea and stabbed the straw in.

Coldly, she said, "Didn't you choose Luna Stewart? Why come back to find me?"

"When did I choose her?" Finn Carter stretched lazily.

"Heh, she's bigger than me, more flirtatious than me, better at acting cute than me! Don't all you men love that!" Aria Stewart sneered.

"What's the use of being big!" Finn Carter blinked. "Can she grow a twenty-plus millimeter lump like you? You're the impressive one!"

Aria Stewart nearly choked with anger, gritting her teeth.

Thank you for the compliment!!

The next moment, she suddenly realized.

Finn Carter had slipped up!

Earlier, she only said it was a friend with chest pain.

She never mentioned a lump!

You bastard, still dare to say you don't know medicine?!

Fine, keep pretending!

I'll make sure your true colors show one day!

Aria Stewart bit down hard on the straw and took a fierce sip.

"Ugh... cough cough cough..." The intensely bitter taste made her almost vomit.

"Finn Carter! Is this fit for human consumption?!"

Finn Carter closed his eyes to rest, casually saying, "Drink it or don't. Don't regret it if you don't."

Furious, Aria Stewart was about to throw it in his face when she heard his inner thoughts.

Ungrateful old woman.

I personally gathered the medicine.

Used internal energy to brew it quickly for you.

Personally delivered it to your hands.

Is there anyone more professional providing full service than me?

Don't drink it then.

Keep your twenty-plus millimeter lump as a family heirloom.

Aria Stewart fell silent.

She gripped the cup tightly, looking at Finn Carter with complex emotions.

In her heart, she actually felt a trace of warmth she hadn't felt in a long time...

She didn't speak again.

Enduring the strong bitterness, she drank the "milk tea" sip by sip.

"Where to next?" Aria Stewart suddenly asked.

"What kind of question is that." Finn Carter scoffed. "I'm your Lin family's live-in son-in-law. Where we go is up to you!"

The resentment in his tone was obvious.

He clearly held a grudge about the live-in marriage.

For some reason, seeing him so aggrieved made Aria Stewart feel a little happy.

Just then, Aria Stewart's phone suddenly rang.

"President Lin, where are you? Zhao Chuanping is at the company. He says there's a problem with the land in the southern suburbs!"

Aria Stewart frowned. "Understood. I'll be right back."

After hanging up, she pondered for a moment. "Come back to the company with me first."
