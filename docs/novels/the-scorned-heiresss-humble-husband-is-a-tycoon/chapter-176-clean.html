Chapter 176

Everyone in the fashion industry who understood the inner workings knew the truth.

Samuel’s scheme was destined for a spectacular downfall.

He kept facing humiliation, repeatedly outmaneuvered by Elise.

All he could do now was clench his fists, his furious gaze fixed on Elise through the screen.

The host’s voice cut through the tension. "We are all now convinced that Elise is indeed Lovely. We are deeply honored she chose to participate in this competition. However, the rules explicitly state that all designs must be created on the spot. Any form of imitation is strictly forbidden. The judges and production team have reached a unanimous decision. Even if Elise used one of her own previous designs, it still constitutes plagiarism. Therefore, she will be disqualified from the competition—"

"But I did create a new dress," Elise stated firmly, interrupting him.

The host was visibly startled.

Elise repeated her claim, her voice clear and resonant. "I designed and constructed a dress here, live. Someone switched it."

The studio erupted into chaos once more.

This was a reality show, not a scripted drama! The endless twists were overwhelming.

Yet, the audience was electrified, their excited cheers filling the air. The live comment section exploded.

[My heart can't handle this suspense!]

[This is a total bloodbath! The drama is unreal!]

[I'm literally on the edge of my seat! I just wanted to watch a show, not have a heart attack!]

[This is the most authentic reality TV has ever been! No way this is scripted! Go, Elise, go!]

The host quickly regained his composure after a moment's pause. "Ms. Scott, are you asserting that you designed and made a dress, but Amanda ended up wearing a different one?"

Amanda’s face drained of color at the sudden accusation.

Initially, Amanda had been furious that Elise revealed another of her hidden talents. She had always been intensely jealous of women more accomplished than herself. Elise’s remarkable success at Mitchell Corp only highlighted Amanda’s own inadequacies.

Amanda had despised Elise for a long time. When Sophia expressed interest in Samuel, Amanda eagerly facilitated their connection.

Sophia had been Amanda’s sycophantic friend for years, constantly showering her with praise and favors. Amanda relished that adoration.

A woman like Elise, who treated her with mere neutrality, deserved to be discarded by William.

But now, panic set in as she was publicly implicated in a plot against Elise.

She wanted to defend herself, but didn't know how. Lashing out on camera would only make things worse.

Elise, however, was brutally direct. "If my suspicion is correct, my backstage assistant, Nana, was the one who switched the dresses."

Nana was standing near the backstage entrance, on standby.

Every camera in the studio instantly swiveled toward her.

Instinctively, she threw her hands up to cover her face—a clear, guilty reaction captured for all to see.
