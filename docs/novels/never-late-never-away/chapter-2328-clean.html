Chapter 2328

His gentleness warmed Joan’s heart like the morning sun. Nonetheless, she saw her relationship with Dustin as strictly platonic.

“I’m really impressed by how well-prepared you are!” she complimented him with an exaggerated laugh to ease the awkwardness between them.

Little did she know he had specifically prepared the scarf for her.

“How about we go for a pizza? If I’m not mistaken, the pizza joint has just come up with their new menu!”

“Sure, let’s go!” Dustin replied gleefully.

Both of them left the supermarket in high spirits and headed for the pizza joint not far from them.

Probably due to the chilly weather, the pizza joint was more packed than usual.

“Waiter, I want to add two appetizers!”

“Waiter, get me the tabasco!”

“Five cans of beer, please!”

The place was crowded, but everyone was having a good time.

“Look, there’s a vacant table in the corner. Is it fine for you?” Dustin whispered with concern.

He knew Joan was an introvert who preferred a quieter environment.

“Yeah, let’s take the seat.” She made a beeline for the table.

Dustin was in a good mood lately. Having Joan to himself like tonight only made things better.

Norton Corporation would be his soon. He wondered what Larry would do when he loses his company. I guess it’s only a matter of time before Larry and Joan file for a divorce. Then she will be mine forever! Dustin couldn’t wait for his dream to come true.

Contrary to the joyous atmosphere at the pizza joint, loneliness shrouded Larry. Sitting on the sofa in his dimly lit office, the haggard man smoked as he stared sorrowfully at the moon.

He was exhausted and lost.

Feeling helpless was a new experience for Larry. Thinking of Joan was driving him insane. He yearned to seek solace in her warm embrace, but he didn’t know where to find her. To make things worse, he had received the divorce papers. His world was falling apart. He knew Delilah wouldn’t send him the papers without Joan’s consent.

“Larry, you haven’t left?” Caspian entered his office and asked softly.

“To where? Home?”

My wife is divorcing me? Do I still have a home? There was a self-deprecating smile on Larry’s face.

“Larry, I’m sure Joan did it in a moment of anger. She just needs more time to come around. When she does, everything will be fine. You’ll see.”

Caspian knew how stressful it was for Larry to resolve the matters in the company. The divorce agreement from Joan was another great blow to him. The challenges had one by one plunged him into a bottomless pit of despair.

“Did you locate the shareholder’s whereabouts?” Larry asked solemnly.

In an instant, Caspian stared at the floor, thinking of a way to break the news.

The other shareholders failed to track him down throughout all these years. How could I do it in such a short time? It’s mission impossible!

“There’s still no sign of him. But Larry, don’t worry. We won’t give up searching for him. Once we find a lead, I’ll notify you immediately.” Caspian tried to cheer him up.

How long do I need to wait? One year? Two? Three years? Perhaps we might never find him again? Larry felt down at the thought.

Caspian had never seen him in such a miserable state. Larry used to be fearless. He would forge ahead even under extreme circumstances. This time, he’s really powerless no matter how hard he tries. There’s nothing much we can do if the shareholder refuses to show up.

“What’s the matter with both of you? It’s almost midnight. Do you intend to stay awake the whole night here? Even machines can’t operate non-stop. Do you want to end up dying of fatigue? Go back to sleep now!” Jessica came and roared at the men.
