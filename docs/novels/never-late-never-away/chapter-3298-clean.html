Chapter 3300 A Terrible Partner

Before their seats were even warm in the OB van, Mia fumed as she dragged Tod over, having just been eliminated.

When Jerry saw her reddened face, his mind was filled with contempt. If it was Osmond who spoke to him, he was at least prepared to show him some respect. But if Mia wanted to do the same, he was simply going to ignore her.

“What’s the problem now? Why do you keep getting into trouble?” Jerry asked.

At that moment, Osmond coincidentally went to the loo. Unable to hold her temper back, Mia pointed angrily at Tod.

She complained, “This man isn’t a partner. He doesn’t care about me and caused me to be eliminated again. I don’t care! I demand a reshoot!”

While everyone present was stunned by her words, Megan looked at her as if she was a lunatic. Little did Mia know that Jerry had already done her a big favor by letting her reshoot the first time.

Even if Jacinta were here, she wouldn’t have the authority to ask Jerry for another reshoot. Hence, Mia’s request was simply too audacious.

“Mia, I feel that you may not be suited for variety shows.”

Trying hard to suppress his anger, Jerry’s gaze was so sharp that it could cut. However, Mia ignored the look in his eyes.

As Osmond had arm-twisted Jerry earlier, Mia felt the director lacked any real authority. Hence, there was no reason for her to treat him with respect.

“What’s the problem? Since you have reshot once, why can’t you do it again?”

Triggered by Mia’s impudence, Jerry’s expression darkened while his heart pounded in rage.

Why does such an insensitive person even exist? Does Mia even have a brain?

“Mia, why are you out here?”

At that moment, Osmond returned from the loo. When he saw the five of them at the OB van, he couldn’t help but narrow his eyes.

It seems something must have happened. She just can’t stop getting into trouble.

“Osmond! They are ganging up against me! After just filming for half an hour, they tried their best to get me eliminated.” The moment she saw Osmond, Mia seemed to have found her courage. Letting go of Tod’s hand, she put on a pitiful expression while storming out of the van.

Osmond wasn’t surprised by Mia’s words. When he saw the smug expressions on everyone’s faces, he sneered, “Don’t you dare mess around with me. This is nothing but a lowly variety show and yet, you are ganging up against her. Since we are not welcomed here, we will leave.”

Despite the animosity in his tone, Osmond was just trying to pave a way for Mia and him to leave without being humiliated.

After all, he was well aware that it would be impossible to ask for another reshoot. When he saw the look on everyone’s faces, it was obvious to him what had just happened.

Hence, he knew it was necessary to put on some airs so that they wouldn’t look bad while quitting the show.

Unfortunately, Mia was too dumb to understand the undercurrents of the situation. She had assumed that Osmond was just trying to stand up for her.

“Did you hear that? We don’t care about your lousy show. You had better hurry up and beg us to stay now!”

Her insolent words caused Osmond’s eyes to narrow. That was when he realized that she was the epitome of a terrible partner.

“Can you just shut up for once?”

Inside the OB van, Megan was amused by the exasperated look on Osmond’s face.

Despite being extremely capable, he was still unable to support an idiot like Mia.

“Enough, stop disgracing yourself and go back to the office at once!”

Osmond had wanted to help her leave with her pride intact. However, given how stupid she was, it would be enough for him that she wasn’t being ridiculed on the way out.

“But I…” Feeling aggrieved, she swept her gaze around the OB van. As anger and jealousy filled her heart, her feet were so heavy that she was unable to move them at all.
