“We’re just friends. Please don’t make any false assumptions about us. To me, he’s just a junior who had saved my life before. I really don’t have any other feelings for him,” Joan continued to explain herself.

“B*tch, did you really think that I have no idea what you’ve done to him? When you guys were in college, all he could think of every day was you. Even after he graduated and went overseas, you were still the one he loved. Because of you, I have to wait for him until now!” Noelle bellowed, boiling with rage.

Joan could not help but scoff. What does she mean? When we were in college, I was not even close with him—we were just acquaintances. Why did she speak of me as if I was a slut?

“Let me repeat myself again: there is nothing going on between Caiden and me. Not then, not now, not ever. If you still don’t believe me, you can always go and ask Caiden yourself. I’ve got to go.” Joan then turned on her heel and left.

“Stop where you are! Joan, you wicked woman!” Noelle roared, struggling to get away from Jaden.

“That’s enough!” Suddenly, someone bellowed from upstairs.

Noelle collected herself and turned around slowly to the voice.

“Caiden, why didn’t you come out just now? You’re clearly at home.” She pouted.

Why do I have to come out? To get myself annoyed? To get myself to be tortured by her clinginess? After so many years, Caiden had never gotten the courage to tell Noelle that he really disliked her. Ever since they were young, she had always been chasing after him and clinging to him. And he hated it.

“Noelle, why are you here? If you have a problem with me, we need to talk it out. But if you’re just here to visit me, then you shall leave now,” Caiden spoke calmly and determinedly.

How can he do this to me? Noelle stared at the man she loved, eyes brimming with tears. Why is he acting so distant now? He was not such a cold person before. When we were young, he would always pamper me and let me do whatever I wanted. Why is he becoming like this?

Seeing Noelle shedding tears, Jaden grumbled in dissatisfaction, “Caiden, don’t be so harsh on her. She’s just concerned about you.”

“I don’t need her to care about me. In fact, I don’t need anyone to be concerned about me. All of these ‘concerns’ bring me nothing but annoyance!” Caiden raised his voice all of a sudden.

Noelle was stunned.

It turns out that he has always disliked and even hated me.

“Caiden, what should I do to make you happy then?” Noelle questioned tentatively, sniffling.

Without a second of hesitation, he replied, “All you need to do is stay away from me. The further, the better.”

Jaden’s eyes widened in shock. Even though Caiden and I have been friends since childhood, it doesn’t mean that he can hurt my sister like that.

“Caiden, you’ve gone too far!” Jaden yelled back.

However, Caiden merely sneered in response. He had long been tired of such life. Now, he preferred to live alone.

“That was all I wanted to say. Both of you can do whatever you want. I don’t want to waste any more time.” He swept a glance over them before going back into his room.

Meanwhile, in a building overseas, a man was sitting in a meeting room, having a discussion with a big company’s chairman. Their conversation was not very pleasant at first, but after ten minutes, the two of them seemed to be engaged in jovial exchanges.

“Larry, I didn’t know that you’re such a committed man.” The chairman laughed.

“Actually, every man has his romantic side…” As Larry continued to share his story, the chairman paid his full attention and was totally engrossed in it.

The chairman never had the intention to revoke the deal with Norton Corporation. He just needed Larry to travel here and held him up on behalf of someone.

“Okay, if that’s the case, it seems like I was not very clear with your company’s background previously. After listening to your explanation, I finally understand it now. The information given by my staff is not so comprehensive yet. I’ll reconsider the contract between your company and mine,” the chairman said and rose to his feet, extending his right hand to Larry.
