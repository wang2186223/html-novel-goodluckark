In order to prevent Mia from rising to fame, all Megan needed to do was to cut off any potential endorsements.

Yet, Megan was not in a particularly favorable position.

Not only was she a lowly assistant, but she also did not have the fame nor looks.

The investors would be utter fools if they allowed her to shoot the commercial.

There were still six months before Mia would obtain the commercial.

it's February now...

A brilliant idea struck Megan's mind.

All of a sudden, she recalled the famous drama, Transcendent, that had begun airing in October previously.

Transcendent rose to extreme fame in the drama industry.

In fact, its fame was on par with Friends from A Nation.

It was considered one of Felix's most famous works.

If she had the chance to star in the drama, Megan was sure that she wouldn't land the role of the female lead nor a likable character.

If she wanted to leave a memorable impact on the audience, the role of a villainess would suit her perfectly.

The second villainess in the drama was particularly memorable as the audience came to hate her with a burning passion.

Her wickedness resembled the Devil himself.

Besides, part of Transcendent's explosive popularity could be associated to the superb acting skills of the antagonist.

Because of her immersive and realistic acting, the audience felt genuinely terrified when the female lead was tortured by the villainess.

Megan had done an analysis of the character of Princess Madeline in her past life.

The actress who played the role was relatively unknown before the drama.

Instead, she relied on her solid acting skills and became an international sensation with the release of this drama.

In the original novel, Princess Madeline had a very extensive description.

Although she was an antagonist, the author had written the character with detailed descriptions.

As a result, the character stood out in the story.

Princess Madeline gained both fans and haters due to her pitiful backstory; she had become a villain after being mercilessly bullied by others.

If she could land the role, Megan wouldn't have to worry about endorsements anymore.

Yet, Megan was aware that she could not participate in the drama with her current status.

Even if she rebirthed again, it would be difficult for her to even play the role of a background character, let alone the main villainess.

"What on earth is wrong with this director? I should just quit."

Jacinta seethed in anger as she threw another tantrum.

The crew couldn't meet Jacinta's acting requirements as her schedule was already arranged until next year.

Hearing Jacinta's statement, Megan thought of Felix.

I might have a chance if I earn Felix's recommendation! As Megan had saved Felix's number in her phone, she quickly contacted him.

Megan had managed to purchase a phone by taking out part of her savings.

Although the phone wasn't particularly modern, it contained basic Internet functions.

The software had also preinstalled Twitter.

These apps were more than sufficient.

Felix? Hearing his name, Megan beamed in delight.

How could I have forgotten about Felix? Megan had been pondering over the casting selection for a few days now.

She didn't have any fame nor did she recognize anyone powerful in the acting industry.

How am I supposed to land the role of Princess Madeline? Yet, Jacinta's tantrum reminded her of Felix.

He had always given her special treatment.

With that, Megan made the decision to contact Felix.

As a person working in showbiz, it was difficult to obtain the number of a director.

Megan stared at the strings of numbers on her phone before she made the hefty decision to call him.

Yet, her phone call went to voicemail after a long moment.

Despite that, Megan did not give up.

Instead, she drafted a short message: Dear Felix, I'd like to ask about the cast for the upcoming drama - Transcendent.

Do you know if they ate in the midst of selecting the cast right now? Without rambling on, Megan cut straight to the chase.

Right at that moment, Felix was in the midst of a grand banquet.

With a smile, he patted on the shoulder of the director responsible for Transcendent.

"Allow me to introduce a person for the role of Princess Madeline! I'm confident that she'll be the perfect cast!"

On the other hand, Jacinta went on a strike after she received the call as her requests weren't met.
