Chapter 1358

As the car screeched to a halt, Fabian had arrived at the entrance of the Jackson Group skyscraper.

“Mr. Norton, we are here,” Fabian’s driver turned around and informed him.

Without saying a word, Fabian nodded thoughtfully. During the journey, he was trying to figure out what William and Waylon were conspiring to achieve. In the end, he came to a conclusion – those two were trying to take over the company.

However, Fabian was surprised by the idea itself. Are those two alone enough to even do it? Isn’t it preposterous? Nevertheless, Fabian was inexplicably certain about their agenda. Or else, they wouldn’t have wanted him dead.

Furthermore, there had to be someone even more powerful controlling them from behind the scenes. And this mysterious person was the true mastermind behind the plot to topple him.

Shaking his head, Fabian smiled wryly as he opened the door and alighted.

No matter who it is, anyone that challenges me will be punished by the heavens. After all, I am favored by the divine.

“Sir, may I know who it is you’re here to see?”

A security guard stopped him when he was about to enter.

“I’m here to see your chairman.”

Fabian was displeased. How dare you stop me when Xavier Jackson was the one who invited me.

“Do you have an appointment?” the security guard enquired.

Based on ground rules, no one was allowed to see the chairman without a prior appointment. If anyone could go in and see him, wouldn’t the chairman be overwhelmed?

“Yes, I do,” Fabian replied plainly.

“Okay. May I know your name and which company are you from? Also, please include your designation so that I can inform the chairman’s secretary.”

“Just get it from the person behind me.”

The moment he quipped, Fabian walked right through the entrance. Now that I’m here, how dare Xavier not welcome me personally? Isn’t he being rude? Since you don’t really care, I’ll suit myself then.

The security guard nodded as Fabian didn’t seem to be lying. Hence, he asked the person behind, “May I know his name, workplace, and designation?”

Fabian’s driver was about the age of his own father and was someone very experienced. Realizing that Fabian was peeved, he smiled at the security guard. “Come, my friend, I’ll save you all the trouble. This is the confirmation of our appointment. Take a look here.”

Just as he spoke, Fabian’s driver clenched his fist as if he was holding the appointment confirmation within it.

Despite feeling frustrated, the guard lowered his head and looked at the driver’s fist.

The very moment he did, Fabian’s driver launched an uppercut into the guard’s face. Upon impact, the guard was already seeing stars.

“Quick… stop him…”

Before he could finish, the guard collapsed onto the ground. What a joke. I am actually good with my hands unlike you, you useless little f*cker.

With that, Fabian’s driver quickly caught up with him.

At the next moment, another security started yelling when he saw his colleague sprawled on the ground. “Stop those suspicious men!”

He screamed as he charged in Fabian’s direction.

“You, f*ckers, are the ones that are suspicious!” Colton barked in return at the security guard. He was obviously someone with a bad temper.

When Fabian stepped into the main lobby, he saw that it was bustling with activity. Just the security guards alone numbered more than ten.

“Guys, surround those two suspicious men” the security guard yelled from behind, pointing at them.

He was panting heavily from having to run after Fabian and his companion.

Suddenly, the crowd was in an uproar as they were curious as to who Fabian was. After all, he had brazenly stormed into Jackson Group with the intention of causing trouble.
