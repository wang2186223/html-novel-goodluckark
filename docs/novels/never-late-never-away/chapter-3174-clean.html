Chapter 3174

"It's going up the trending list swiftly!"

Donna stared at the number one trending topic in shock.

Mia was just a rookie starlet.She was nowhere as famous as Sandra Holm.

The reason she could become the number one search topic was that Rocky posted a juicy preview a few days back that piqued everyone's interest.

"That's perfectly normal." Megan was unfazed.

She stood up and glanced at the calendar before telling Donna.

"This afternoon, we shall head to the studio at Sky Entertainment"

After three whole months, Megan is finally stepping out of her house? Donna stood up in shock as she uttered curiously, "Why are we heading there?"

Megan flashed a satisfied smirk.

She told Donna honestly, "To shoot a commercial, of course.

According to Jake's schedule, which Rocky provided to me, I found out they are hiring extras today."

"Extras?"

Donna's face fell.

She furrowed her brows and wailed, "Seriously? You used to act in a TV drama.Why would you want to be an extra now?"

Seeing how dramatic Donna's reaction was, Megan immediately realized the former had no idea what her plan was.

The corners of her lips lifted in a confident grin as she announced, "You'll know why when we get there.Getting the role isn't my goal."

Around ten minutes later, they arrived at a studio under Wilson Group.

There was a long queue in front of the entrance, showing how important this commercial was to them.

"Hey, isn't that Mia?"

Donna tugged on Megan's sleeves and pointed at the gap between the curtains.

Mia's pure and gorgeous side profile was glowing under the studio lights.

"Oh?"

Megan glanced at her disinterestedly as her focus returned to the queue she was in.

"Doesn't that bother you, Megan?"

Donna couldn't help but shudder in fear at the thought of what happened that day when they moved houses.She couldn't understand why Megan was this calm.

Right now, Megan's attention was focused on Jake.

His schedule stated he would be here, but after peeking at the interior of the studio a few times, she still couldn't spot him anywhere.

"Why would I be bothered?"

Finally, it was her turn.

Megan easily got through the preliminary as she was rather decent-looking.

She took her number and entered the studio with a staff while Donna waited for her outside.

If she had remembered correctly, this commercial shoot was the turning point of Mia's career.

As it was the first commercial in the industry to include a storyline introducing the product, it was considered a classic.

Mia played the role of a blind girl who was mocked mercilessly by others.

She dreamed of performing on stage, but the judges drenched her with water and hurled insults at her.

Megan was here today as an extra playing either the role of the audience or the judge.

Jake wasn't here yet, so Megan couldn't help but notice Mia, who was currently under the spotlight.

The latter was still preparing herself before the shooting began.

"This way.The four of you will be the judges.The rest will be the audience"

As the studio was bustling with people, the director raised his loud hailer and gathered the extras.

He picked a few of them who looked more mature to be the judges.

Coincidentally, one of them was the very distracted Megan.

"Hurry, take your seats.We're about to start!"

Megan was still waiting for Jake and wasn't interested in the commercial at all.

She kept glancing around anxiously.

The minute Mia stepped onto the stage, she spotted Megan in the judges’ panel.

As the spotlight illuminated her entire being, her lips parted in surprise.

She swiftly regained her composure and stormed off the stage to grab a preoccupied Megan's hand.

"Why are you here?"

Mia demanded furiously.

As the pain jolted up her arm, Megan snapped back to reality and saw Mia standing in front of her.

Despite knowing a confrontation with Mia would happen anytime, she was still stunned to see Mia confronting her in public without hesitation.
