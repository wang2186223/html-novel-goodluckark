[Ms. Worry-Free needs to keep it up too! Let's see whose love is sweeter!]

The netizens were initially just admiring their looks tonight.

On one side, you had Ava, the rising star in the entertainment world,known for her beauty and kind heart, which earned her a massive fanbase.To fans. Ava and Arthur were the perfect couple.

On the other side, Ella didn't have a huge following, but her fans were fierce with their words.

The director stood off to the side, grinning like a Cheshire cat.

The more they argued, the more popular the live stream got.

He couldn't stop smiling at the sight.

Ella was oblivious to all this. She wandered into the crowd, grabbed a piece of cake, and took a bite. "This is really good, so delicious!"

Now it seemed she was overthinking it.

Realizing this, she laughed heartily. "Arthur, let's go barbecue!"

"Okay! You go ahead."

Arthur said casually, then walked in the other direction towards Ella.

Ava looked annoyed and wanted to follow, but someone blocked her way.

Everything was perfect: tasty food, fine wine, and dazzling lights.

Ella held a glass of juice, looking a bit regretful. "It's a pity I can't drink today. otherwise, I'd definitely drink to my heart's content!"

"With your body, you still want to get drunk!"

Seeing someone approaching, Lily raised an eyebrow. "The opportunity is right in front of you. don't miss it!"

"What?"

Ella hadn't figured out what was going on when Lily turned and left.

She was still standing there blinking when a coat with a familiar scent was draped over her shoulders.

The coat still had Arthur's warmth.

She was stunned, and just as she was about to turn around, Arthur's solid chest pressed against her.

Even through two layers of fabric, she could feel his burning body."Dressed like this, what are you trying to do? Meeting another man?"

Arthur's last words were slow and hoarse, with a hint of threat.

His breath enveloped her tightly. Ella trembled all over and hadn't yet explained when his other hand grabbed her waist under the cover of the wide coat.

"I didn't! And there's nothing wrong with my outfit!"

Clearly, everyone was dressed provocatively,so why single her out alone?

Ella felt indignant and wanted to say more, but the hand on her waist tightened, holding her firmly in his arms.

"Long legs, high slit, and an open back."

With each word, the surrounding air grew colder.

In the scorching summer, Ella even felt like she was falling into an ice cave.her hair standing on end. She subtly shifted, attempting to distance herself from him.

But Arthur's hand was lke a clamp on her waist.

"Don't move,or else..."

The threat was clear.

Even without turning around, Ella could imagine Arthur's cold gaze.

She stood there obediently. "If you don't like it, I'll change next time!"

"These clothes are only for me to see!"

As he spoke, his hand wandered over her smooth back and slender waist,

finally resting on her long, beautiful legs. Wherever his hand touched, it left a burning sensation.

The two stood there in an close posture, and the photographer immediately focused the camera on them.

From a distance, their conversation couldn't be heard clearly, but their posture was suggestive.

In the dim light. Ella was wrapped up tightly, her face obscured,but she leaned weakly against Arthur.

Arthur, on the other hand, held her in his embrace.

He tilted his head close to her ear, whispering like lovers.

The viewers in the live stream couldn't stay calm after seeing this.

[They're clearly not doing anything, but it feels like they're doing everything! Where is Mr. Smith hand? What's he doing!]

[This is too suggestive; I am even watching this for free!]

[Some people are going to cry!]

Every woman cared about her appearance.

He thought. 'Come on, speak up, boost the ratings.

Meanwhile, Ella was trembling all over. Just as Arthur was about to kiss her earlobe, her body went weak, almost falling to the ground.

Fortunately, Arthur quickly held her waist. "You're too sensitive!"

"You!"

Ella. angry like a ruffled cat, pushed him away forcefully,but caught a glimpse of Lily's situation, her face changing.

"You'd better not protect Ava in everything."

Leaving a sentence, she quickly ran away in high heels.

In her haste, the coat on her shoulder fell to the ground.

Arthur stood rooted to the spot, gazing at the smooth expanse of her back.His throat tightened, his jaw clenched, and a look of displeasure crossed his face.
