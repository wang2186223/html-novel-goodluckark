Title：
Ruthless Redemption: The Billionaire Ex‘s Second Chance

Intro：
Eleanor Thompson's life was turned upside down when she discovered her husband, Nathaniel Carter, was involved with another woman. After years of a loveless marriage, she decides to divorce him, only to find herself caught in a web of family politics and betrayal. As she navigates through the complexities of her past and present, Eleanor must fight for her freedom and reclaim her identity. With her resilience and determination, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment, refusing to be defined by her circumstances.
